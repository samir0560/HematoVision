üéØ Project Overview

HematoVision is an advanced blood cell classification system that utilizes transfer learning with MobileNetV2 to accurately identify and classify different types of blood cells. The system is designed to assist pathologists and healthcare professionals in automated blood analysis.

üìä Supported Cell Types

  • Eosinophil - Granulocyte with bi-lobed nucleus
  • Lymphocyte - Small white blood cell
  • Monocyte - Largest type of white blood cell
  • Neutrophil - Most abundant white blood cell

üìà Performance Metrics

The model is trained on 12,500 annotated blood cell images and achieves high accuracy through:

  • Data augmentation for improved generalization
  • Transfer learning from ImageNet pre-trained weights
  • Proper train/validation split (80/20)
  • Early stopping and learning rate scheduling
